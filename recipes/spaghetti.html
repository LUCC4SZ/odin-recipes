Milanesa

Description

A traditional spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato juice
  • Onion
  • Red pepper
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Parsley
  • Salt

Steps

  • Put some water to boil.
  • Chop half an onion, a string of red pepper and a garlic clove.
  • On a frying pan cook it until the onion gets a golden-like color.
  • Add tomato juice.
  • Put the spaghetti on the boiling water.
  • Wait for the spaghetti to be al dente.
  • Pour the spaghetti.
  • Pour with the sauce the spaghetti.
  • Enjoy your meal!
