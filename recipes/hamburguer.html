Hamburguer

Description

A delicious and healthy homemade hamburguer.

Ingredients

  • Minced meat
  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Red pepper
  • Green pepper
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  • Break an egg and put it in a bowl.
  • Chop half an onion and a string of both peppers.
  • On a frying pan, cook the onion and pepper.
  • Add the onion and pepper to the bowl. Also put the meat in.
  • Add salt and oregano.
  • Start mixing everything using your hands. Make sure to mix it well.
  • Add a little bit of breadcrumb in the bowl.
  • Mix once again.
  • Grab a considered amount of meat and start shaping it like a small ball. Then flatten it.
  • On a tray pour a little bit of oil and place the hamburguers.
  • Put the tray in the oven and wait for 20 minutes.
  • Flip the hamburguers and wait for 15 minutes.
  • Enjoy your meal!
